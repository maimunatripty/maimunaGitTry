Lichi, commonly known as lychee, is a tropical fruit originating from the Guangdong and Fujian provinces of China. Renowned for its sweet and aromatic flavor, lychee has a rough, reddish outer skin that encases a translucent, juicy flesh with a single large seed. This fruit is not only delicious but also rich in nutrients, including vitamin C, B-complex vitamins, and various antioxidants, making it a healthy addition to one's diet. Lychees are often enjoyed fresh, in desserts, or as part of beverages, adding a burst of exotic flavor. Their unique taste and numerous health benefits have made lychees a popular fruit worldwide.

Mango, often hailed as the "king of fruits," is a tropical stone fruit known for its sweet and tangy flavor, originating from South Asia. Its vibrant orange flesh, encased in a smooth, sometimes fibrous skin, is rich in vitamins A and C, dietary fiber, and a variety of antioxidants. Mangoes can be enjoyed fresh, dried, or in various culinary creations, such as smoothies, salads, and desserts. They are celebrated not only for their delicious taste but also for their numerous health benefits, including boosting immunity and improving digestion. This beloved fruit's versatility and nutritional value have made it a favorite in many cultures around the globe.
